Healthcare is expensive, or is it?

The main reason we experience high healthcare costs is because prices for healthcare services are not available before we receive care. We find out how much we’re required to pay after we’ve already received care. We cannot where we get care based on price. If we could do so, we would choose lower priced providers. And healthcare would not seem as expensive.

Don't know the price? Let's work around that together

While doctor prices are not usually available, we can avoid utilizing high-cost providers with shopMD .
The difference between high- and low-cost providers can be 50%-90%. In other words, something that costs a few hundred dollars at one provider could cost a few thousand dollars at another.
shopMD is the easiest way to circumvent the price transparency barrier in healthcare. We do this by exposing low-cost specialty providers across the US.

Why Specialty Providers?

Specialty care is expensive to start with. By reducing your cost of specialty care, y ou can have the biggest impact on your healthcare spend.

  • Specialty care generates high costs for patients.
  • Specialtysts’ prices are higher than primary care doctors.
  • Specialty care is a more involved patient journey that can require follow up visits, tests and procedures that add costs cumulatively. Specialty care is mostly considered non-preventative for insurance purposes, and not covered as well under most commercial insurance plans.

Timing is Everything!

Another reason patients can save a lot by taking control of their specialty care journey is that specialty care encounters can be planned.
 Unlike emergent care or sudden hospitalization from unexpected events, most specialty care is outpatient and can be planned in advance. This gives patients time to decide how and where they choose to get care. It gives them the time and opportunity to explore low-cost options on shopMD.
