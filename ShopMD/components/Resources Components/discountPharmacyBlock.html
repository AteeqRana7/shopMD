Looking for Discounted Pharmacies?

There are many options to save on drug prices by shopping around. Even if your insurance covers prescription drugs, you may find that it costs less to purchase some drugs using self-pay than going through insurance:

Why a patient paid a $285 copay for a $40 drug Prescription Drugs May Cost More With Insurance Than Without It

In addition to online pharmacies or coupon vendors listed below, you should also check prices at your local discount stores such as Costco or Walmart to see if they offer lower prices. If you have a local independent pharmacy, you may also start with them as they are likely to have lower prices than large pharmacy chains.

The list of online discount pharmacies is continuing to grow. Amazon is also starting to offer certain pharmaceuticals by mail order.

Another way to save on prescription drugs is to ask your doctor or pharmacist if a generic alternative is available for the drug you need. These are priced lower than brand name drugs of the same formulation. Big box stores like Walmart, Target and Costco have specific programs offering low price generic drugs.
