Looking for Primary Care?

Studies have shown that having a primary care doctor and getting regular and preventative primary care improves wellbeing and long term health. If you have insurance, most preventative care should be covered with a copay and you should be able to get a primary care doctor through your plan. If you do not have insurance, Direct Primary Care (DPC) doctors offer reasonable monthly subscription plans that takes care of primary care and some urgent care needs. You can search the internet for ‘Direct Primary Care near me’ and be able to locate DPC practices.
